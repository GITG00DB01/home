Best Apex Characters

The legends of the apex games are all versitile in their own ways, this list is on my personal opion so please dont take these as an actual list made my the people at EA and Respawn

1. Caustic

The physco who murdered everyone in his path in the name of "science", caustic's gas traps are great at cutting off his enemys and good as a form of a deadly smoke screen to revive his teamates or kill his enemys. He is one of the two people with the Fortified attribute (10% damage reduction) making him a walking tank with the right gear

2. Octane

An adrenaline junkie at heart. Octane is one of the newer addition (after he came out in season 1), he is extremely deadly with his speed. He takes a form of a stimulant that increases his speed even more but at the cost of a bit of his health but its not a problem he has to worry about because he has a supprising healing factor that no other legends have

3. Gibraltar

Gibraltar is the other heavy of Apex alongside Caustic. A true defender at heart, Gibraltar has a dome sheild to protect his teammates from enemies and explosions. His teammates can even heal faster while in his dome making him a good part of any team

4. Lifeline

Lifeline is the medic of the apex games, she carries a drone around with her to heal her and her teammates that she calls "D.O.C". she can use it after or even during a fire fight, making it much easier to survive those extra shots.

5. Bloodhound

Bloodhound is a mysterious character because we dont know anything about them, not even their gender. Their ability lets them track anyone within a (estimated) range of 30 to 40 meters all around them, even past caustic and bangalore's smoke.
